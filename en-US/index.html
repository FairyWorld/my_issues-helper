      Here is a very simple and commonly used example. The corresponding scenario is: when an issue adds the help wanted tag, the system will automatically comment.

      name: Issue Reply
      on:
        issues:
          types: [labeled]
      jobs:
        reply-helper:
          runs-on: ubuntu-latest
          steps:
            - name: help wanted
              if: github.event.label.name == 'help wanted'
              uses: actions-cool/issues-helper@v2.1.1
              with:
                actions: 'create-comment'
                token: ${{ secrets.GITHUB_TOKEN }}
                issue-number: ${{ github.event.issue.number }}
                body: |
                  Hello @${{ github.event.issue.user.login }}. We totally like your proposal/feedback, welcome PR„ÄÇ
